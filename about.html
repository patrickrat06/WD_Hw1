About Us

Our Story

A few years ago, we saw how the popularity of halloween started shrinking. Our founder that adores the holiday wanted to take it upon themselves to help halloween gain straction again. They then founded "The Boo Bakery" to combine their love for baking and halloween.

What We Do

We want to inspire kids and teens to enjoy halloween, as it is one of the more unique holidays, and experiencing it as a young person is truly a magical feeling that we don't want to get lost.

Our Process

Our business is defined by how we fund one half of it with the profits from the other.

  • The Bakery

    Firstly we bake our products and we sell it at our locations or online.

  • The Costumes

    With the profits from those sales we invest in creating costumes for our customers.

  • The Balance

    This way we can have a sustainable business that can offer both services, the food and the boo!
